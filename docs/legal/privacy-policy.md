# Privacy Policy

**Tango Finance**

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

## Introduction

Tango Finance ("we," "our," or "us") operates an internal finance management application (the "Service") designed exclusively for use by Tango Agency and its affiliated businesses. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service.

**Important:** This Service is not available to the general public. Access is restricted to authorized employees and representatives of Tango Agency and its affiliated entities.

## Information We Collect

### Information from Connected Services

When you connect third-party services (such as QuickBooks Online, banking providers, or invoice management systems), we collect:

- **Financial Data:** Invoice information, transaction records, account balances, and payment history
- **Business Information:** Company names, tax identification numbers, and contact details
- **Authentication Tokens:** OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to maintain authorized connections to third-party services

### Information You Provide

- Account credentials and authentication information
- Configuration preferences and settings

### Automatically Collected Information

- Access logs and timestamps
- System usage data for debugging and maintenance purposes

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

1. **Financial Management:** Importing, organizing, and reconciling invoices and transactions
2. **Service Operation:** Authenticating connections to third-party services and maintaining data synchronization
3. **Internal Reporting:** Generating financial reports and analytics for authorized business users
4. **System Maintenance:** Debugging issues, ensuring security, and improving the Service

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest
- OAuth tokens are stored securely and used only for authorized API access
- Access to the Service is restricted to authenticated users within our organization
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access

## Third-Party Services

Our Service integrates with third-party platforms including:

- **QuickBooks Online (Intuit):** For invoice and accounting data synchronization
- **Banking Providers:** For transaction data import
- **Invoice Management Systems:** For invoice synchronization

When you authorize these connections, we access only the data necessary for the Service's functionality. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy:
- QuickBooks/Intuit: https://www.intuit.com/privacy/statement/

## Data Retention

We retain financial data for as long as necessary for business operations and legal compliance. OAuth tokens are retained only while the connection to the third-party service remains active. You may request disconnection of any integrated service at any time.

## Your Rights

As an authorized user of this internal Service, you may:

- Request access to your data stored in the Service
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request disconnection of third-party service integrations
- Contact us with privacy-related questions or concerns

## Data Sharing

We do not share your data with external parties except:

- With third-party services you have explicitly authorized (e.g., QuickBooks)
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect the rights and safety of our organization

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated to authorized users through appropriate internal channels.
